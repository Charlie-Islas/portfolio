About me

My name is Charlie Acevedo and I am 26 years old. After studying and working for five years in Germany, I am finally back in Mexico. I have always been a passionate reader and a grammar lover. My years abroad made me realize that a new language can give you as many (if not more) opportunities than a professional degree. This is why I decided to become a language teacher and make the most of my passion for linguistics, grammar, and all that has to do with languages. I also decided to seize my knowledge of chemistry, physics, and maths by offering private lessons on these subjects. And I loved it. After just some months of teaching, I knew that my real passion is teaching.

So I decided to open up my own shop in order to attract even more students. I came up with the idea of creating a coworking space that anyone in Oaxaca can rent in order to use as an office or a classroom. This is how SmartOffices &+ was born, and we can be proud to call it one of the first coworking spaces in Oaxaca. To get to know us more, you can visit our website here.

The wonderful experiences abroad also made me realize that Mexico is an awesome country and that, despite the world being very small, mobility is hard and costly. Among other reasons, I think this was the main one that motivated me to become a web developer. I want to be able to work from whichever point in the globe I am. And I believe that web design is the future. The Internet is an ever-growing market that is supposed to help us connect with each other and break the borders that have divided us as a species for so many centuries. A lot has been made, but there is even much more to come. I believe I can take part in that process and contribute just a bit to make the world a better place for everyone.

CryptoMonstar

All the cryptocurrency information that you need in one single place...

Despite being a trendy topic, consumers are usually not very well informed about cryptocurrencies. Even though there is a lot of information about these topics, it appears that the relevant data and news are spread across the vastness of the Internet. The goal of CryptoMonstar is to present the user with all the information they need to know about cryptocurrencies in one single page.

Cryptomonstar is a dynamic web application that presents the most relevant data about the main cryptocurrencies in the world. In addition, it allows the user to look for additional cryptocurrencies, display news about related topics, play a simple prediction game, and make simple graphic analytics. Visit our website to discover the CryptoMonstar inside you!

Ticketsmarter

A platform to manage all your tickets and events...

An online crowdfunding and ticket management platform for various events. Users can browse and fund events by purchasing tickets or directly contributing to the funding. They can also get their tickets via email or SMS. Event organizers can create events, choose the venue, date, target ticket sales or monetary funding goals, rewards for early pledgers, and much more! They can also emit and validate tickets via QR codes that are easily scanned from a mobile device or a printed version.
